Pooja Ranjan

Herder in chief of Ethereum Cat Herders and Founder of Avarch LLC

She became part of the blockchain community in 2016 by learning about the Ethereum blockchain. Founded Avarch LLC in 2017 with EtherWorld as her first project to create awareness about blockchain technology. Publishing technical content, project updates and latest news, EtherWorld is one of the leading blockchain publishing websites.

In 2020, with the preparation of Ethereum 2.0, BlockAction was launched as an explorer to support Beacon chain multiclient testnet since the launch of Witti testnet. The explorer is currently upgraded to provide information of the Beaconchain mainnet transaction.
